eles/elas

Como escolher o pronome certo?

  • She → mulher ou menina: Mary is a doctor. She works at the hospital.
  • He → homem ou menino: John plays soccer. He is great!
  • It → animal ou objeto: My dog is friendly. It loves to play.
  • They → mais de uma pessoa ou coisa: My parents are home. They are cooking.
💡

Dica prática: Antes de escolher o pronome, identifique quem ou o que está sendo substituído: é masculino, feminino, plural? Isso resolve a maioria das questões!

Simple Present (Presente Simples)

Usado para hábitos, rotinas e fatos gerais

📋 Quando usar?

✅ Hábitos e rotinas: She plays tennis every day.
✅ Fatos permanentes: The sun rises in the east.
✅ Preferências: He likes new blue cars.

🔑 Regra do -S (Terceira pessoa do singular: He / She / It)

Quando o sujeito é he, she ou it, o verbo recebe -s, -es ou -ies no final:

  • play → plays  |  work → works
  • do → does  |  go → goes
  • study → studies  |  carry → carries
Sujeito Verbo (play) Exemplo
I play I play soccer.
You play You play guitar.
He / She / It plays She plays piano. ⭐
We play We play basketball.
You (pl.) play You play volleyball.
They play They play chess.

❓ Perguntas com DO / DOES

Sujeito Auxiliar Exemplo
I / You / We / They Do Do you like pizza?
He / She / It Does Does he like blue cars? ⭐
⚠️

Atenção: Quando usamos DOES na pergunta, o verbo principal volta à forma base (sem -s): Does he likes? ❌  |  Does he like? ✅

🚫 Negativa com DON'T / DOESN'T

I / You / We / They → don't + verbo: They don't own a car.
He / She / It → doesn't + verbo: Mark doesn't own that chair.

Adjective Order (Ordem dos Adjetivos)

A ordem correta dos adjetivos antes do substantivo

📐 Ordem dos Adjetivos em Inglês

→
Origin
Italian

Material
wooden

Macete: O-S-A-S-C-O-M (Opinion · Size · Age · Shape · Color · Origin · Material)

🔍 Exemplos da prova:

We have a small old Italian table.

→ Size (small) → Age (old) → Origin (Italian) ✅


Mark doesn't own that ugly yellow plastic chair.

→ Opinion (ugly) → Color (yellow) → Material (plastic) ✅


My sister often carries a heavy rectangular wooden box.

→ Size (heavy) → Shape (rectangular) → Material (wooden) ✅

🧠

Regra de ouro: Em inglês, os adjetivos vêm ANTES do substantivo e seguem uma ordem específica. Nunca dizemos "box wooden rectangular heavy" — a ordem importa!
